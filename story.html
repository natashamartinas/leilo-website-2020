“we’re on a mission to
de-stress a stressed out world”

our story starts on the south coast of Fiji, where locals have been cultivating and enjoying kava for generations. inspired by the island lifestyle, our founder, Sol, started Leilo with a simple goal in mind: to counterbalance the caffeinated, “work hard, play hard” culture of our time.

⁣ our team spent two years researching and refining our concept, insisting on the best ingredients and sustainable sourcing straight from the south pacific. this quest for perfection led us to a patent-pending, revolutionary product that not only helps people feel good, but they can feel good about drinking in the first place.
⁣
stress shouldn’t have to be constant.
calm is the key to unlocking our full potential… and we make sure it’s only a can away.

our impact

sustainability

our premium noble kava extract is sourced from growers in vanuatu who have sustainably cultivated kava root for thousands of years without fertilizers or pesticides. Leilo is then packaged in the US with 100% recyclable materials, because we’re all about keeping our planet clean!

health conscious

say goodbye to those tired eyes and dreadful hangovers! Leilo was created so you can relax without the sacrifice. using only the best all-natural ingredients, Leilo is a safer alternative to other flawed means of letting loose.

we give back

it's a stressful time, so we've made sure to share relaxation with those who need it most... that's why we've partnered with great organizations like Fuel Our Heroes, Greater Boston Food Bank and the Connecticut Food Bank, spreading calm with each can.
